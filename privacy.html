Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

01Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email, password)
  • Billing information (payment details, billing address)
  • Service usage data and server configurations
  • Communications with our support team

We also automatically collect certain information when you use our services, including IP addresses, browser type, and usage patterns to improve our infrastructure.

02How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process transactions and send related information
  • Send technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity

03Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Strict access controls and authentication
  • 24/7 infrastructure monitoring

04Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We may retain certain information for legitimate business purposes or as required by law.

05Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of marketing communications

06Contact

For privacy-related inquiries or to exercise your rights, contact us at:
